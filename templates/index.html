How it works

A guided purchase journey from plan selection to slot allocation.

The flow is designed to reduce confusion and support a professional payment verification model.

01Review plan

Compare slot value, maturity date, expected return, minimum slots, and early-exit terms.

02Select slots

Choose the number of slots allowed by the plan and confirm the total payable amount.

03Scan QR

A unique QR is generated for the exact request and is valid only for a short time window.

04Payment check

The system waits for provider confirmation or admin verification, depending on your payment setup.

05Slots allocated

After verification, the dashboard shows the confirmed slot allocation and maturity details.

Payment logic

UPI hidden from users, branded QR shown to users.

The receiving UPI ID can be configured in Railway using environment variables. Users see the payee name as YieldNest while scanning. For automatic redirect after payment, connect a payment gateway webhook/status API; otherwise the payment remains under verification.

✓ PLATFORM_UPI_ID controls the receiving UPI account

✓ PLATFORM_PAYEE_NAME controls what appears in UPI apps

✓ Payment webhook can auto-approve and redirect users

✓ Admin can still review requests when needed

Questions

Everything users need before payment.

Will users see my real UPI ID?

No. The payment page shows a branded QR flow. The actual receiving UPI ID is configured from Railway environment variables.

Can the page close automatically after payment?

Yes, when a payment provider webhook or status API confirms success. Without gateway confirmation, a normal UPI QR cannot reliably tell the website that money was received.

Where will allocated slots appear?

After approval, users can open their dashboard to see plan name, slot count, principal amount, expected maturity value, and status.

What if the QR expires?

The QR is valid for 60 seconds. A fresh QR can be generated after the refresh window.
