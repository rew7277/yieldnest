Questions users ask

Important information before proceeding

Is the maturity amount guaranteed?

No. Displayed values are expected calculations based on admin-configured plan rules and must be reviewed with final agreement and applicable law.

What happens if payment is sent but not approved?

The request remains pending or can be rejected if transaction evidence is incomplete, incorrect, or suspicious.

Can a user exit before maturity?

Early withdrawal can attract charges. The charge percentage is shown on the plan page before purchase.

Why is QR valid only for 60 seconds?

Short-lived QR sessions reduce confusion and help each payment request stay linked to a unique purchase session.
